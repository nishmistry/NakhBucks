Welcome to the NakhRAAS Boponomy!

A quick introduction to what the "Boponomy" is:

  • Essentially, people can make bets on anything, with a "bop" (or any number of "bops"), as collateral.
  • If you win the bet, the other person gets that number of "bops" added to their balance. People do not want to have "bops" on their balance. The goal is to get other people to owe more bops!
  • Bops also accrue interest. This means that over time, if someone has any bops in their balance, it'll continually grow. This means that if someone owes 2 bops, and the rate of interest is 50% daily, the next day, they will owe 3 bops.
  • The way for people to reduce bops from their balance is to take a hit. For every hit they take, 1 bop gets removed from their balance.
  • However, there may be a point where people have so many bops on their balance that they are unable to remove bops from their balance as fast as the interest is growing. This means that they have no other choice then to declare "Bopruptcy", which is the equivalent of bankruptcy.
